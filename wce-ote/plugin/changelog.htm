The Online Transcription Editor (OTE) was initiated as part of the joined project "Workspace for Collaborative Editing" and then further developed as part of the joint project MUYA. It was funded by the DFG and the AHRC and the European Research Council (ERC).

This software is based on TinyMCE v4 and is thus published as opensource under the terms of the LGPL v2.1.
